Toward that goal the Lakeland Soccer Club shall organize soccer teams, provide training in the sport of soccer, and receive gifts or donations to promote the health, welfare and recreation of the general public through the sport of soccer.   Charitable Contribution Information Lakeland Soccer Club is a not for profit organization under the Internal Revenue Code section 501(c)(3). Contributions to our club are deductible under secion 170 of the Internal Revenue Code. The tax identification number is available upon request.   Board Meetings Lakeland Soccer Club Board meetings are held on the first Monday of every month, unless the first Monday is a holiday, then in that event the meeting is moved to the second Monday. The meeting times are as follows: March/April/May/September/October/November - 6:30 p.m. Dec/Jan/Feb/June/July/August - 5:00 p.m. All Board meetings are held at the Rathdrum Library conference room.
