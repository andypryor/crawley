    <p><span style="color:#FF0000"><span style="font-size:14px"><strong>Coaches, Parents:&nbsp; BEFORE YOU CALL...</strong></span></span></p>

<p>1. Take a moment to visit our webpages including the <strong><a href="http://www.edmondsoccer.com/page/show/880954-frequently-asked-questions"><span style="color:#0000CD">Frequently Asked Questions</span></a></strong>.&nbsp; Your answer&nbsp; could be at your fingertips.</p>

<p>2<strong>. </strong><a id="coordinators" name="coordinators"><strong>FIND YOUR COORDINATOR BELOW FIRST</strong></a> before you call the club president.&nbsp; Coordinators are here to handle most player and team issues.</p>

<p>ESC is YOUR club! We are organized entirely by parents. As the parent of a child playing on an ESC team, you are entitled to vote for and serve on the board of directors. As a not-for-profit organization, ESC depends on volunteers such as yourself. We need your help with fund raising and tournaments. If you have a skill that will help promote soccer, or if your business would like to sponsor players, or start a project at the club, please volunteer! Contact the&nbsp;<a href="mailto:mailto:gm@edmondsoccer.com">ESC General Manager</a>.&nbsp;<br />
&nbsp;<br />
Board meetings are held at 7 p.m. on the third Tuesday of every month at Sleep Inn, 3608 S. Broadway Extension (next to Outback and Chili&#39;s).</p>
