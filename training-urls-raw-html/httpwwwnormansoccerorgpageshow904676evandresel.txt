    <div class="text clearfix">
    <h3>Evan Dresel</h3>

<p>Staff Coach<br />
Celtic 98, Celtic&nbsp;00</p>

<p><strong>Home:</strong> 405-519-2611<br />
<strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:edresel@macu.edu">edresel@macu.edu</a></p>

<p><strong>Bio:&nbsp;</strong></p>

<p><strong>Evan Dresel is in his&nbsp;seventh season with the MACU soccer programs and&nbsp;fourth season as co-head coach&nbsp;of both the men&#39;s and women&#39;s soccer squads.&nbsp;</strong></p>

<p><strong>Last season, Dresel led the Men&#39;s Soccer Evangels to their best season since joining the NAIA, including their first ever appearance in the Sooner Athletic Conference Postseason Tournament that earned him the NCCAA Central Regional Co-Head Coach of the Year honor. &nbsp;He also helped the Lady Evangels advance to the SAC Tournament for the third straight season that featured a milestone victory over then No. 4-ranked and previously undefeated Oklahoma City University. &nbsp;It would be OCU&#39;s last loss until a setback in the second round of the NAIA National Tournament. &nbsp;</strong></p>

<p><strong>In 2011, Dresel helped keep the Lady Evangels in the top half of the league with 12 victories and second consecutive berth in the SAC Tournament. &nbsp;</strong></p>

<p><strong>Dresel was selected Sooner Athletic Conference Co-Head Coach of the Year after&nbsp;helping the Lady Evangels win a school-record 16 matches along with their best ever finish of third in the Sooner Athletic Conference in the 2010 season.&nbsp; Dresel also helped guide the men&#39;s soccer squad to their third straight NCCAA Central Regional&nbsp;berth and an appearance in the regional semifinals with a&nbsp;resounding 6-0 win over Southwestern Assemblies&nbsp;of God in the quarterfinal round.&nbsp; &nbsp;</strong></p>

<p><strong>Dresel completed his playing career at Southern Nazarene University in 2006. While at SNU he was a 2006 first team All-Conference selection and part of the 2005 Final Four team at the NAIA National Tournament.</strong></p>

<p><strong>Dresel holds his National C Coaching License and is the&nbsp;competitive coach for NYSA Fury 98 and Fury 00 in Norman. &nbsp;He was the assistant soccer coach at his alma mater, Mustang High School from 2002 to 2005. He went on to coach the Mount Saint Mary&#39;s High School men&#39;s team in 2007 and in 2008 he coached both the men&#39;s and women&#39;s program. Also in 2008, Dresel, was an Olympic Development Program Assistant Coach.</strong></p>

<p><strong>Dresel holds a bachelor&#39;s degree in Physical Education from Southern Nazarene University.</strong></p>
