    <h3 style="text-align: center;"><span>MTsc Nation, "Where Kids Come First!"</span></h3>


	    <div class="rightTextImage largeTextImage" style="width:250px; min-height:184px">

      	<a href="http://www.metrotulsasoccer.com/page/show/844677-online-registration" target='_blank'>

      <img alt="" src="http://cdn3.sportngin.com/attachments/text_block/4078/0917/Uncle_Sam_large.png" style="width: 250px" />

      </a>

    
  </div>

    <div class="text clearfix">
    <p>Metro Tulsa Soccer Club (MTsc) offers soccer programs for all&nbsp;levels of skill for youth players ages 2&nbsp;- 19 years old from throughout Oklahoma.&nbsp; All our Programs are open to all youth&nbsp;regardless of where you live. &nbsp;<span style="background-color:rgb(255, 255, 255); color:rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family:lucida grande,verdana,geneva,helvetica,arial,sans; font-size:12px">MTsc offers programs for youth players of all capabilities from special needs to recreational to elite premier state, regional and national level teams.&nbsp;</span>&nbsp;Let your child&#39;s journey start and end with MTsc.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>MTsc&nbsp;Programs Include</strong></p>

<ol>
	<li>Recreational (REC) Random Draw (RD)&nbsp;and Friendship Team (FT)&nbsp;Closed and Open Leagues</li>
	<li>Special Needs League (SNL)</li>
	<li>Player&nbsp;Development (PDP) Skills Training</li>
	<li>Mommy or Daddy &amp; Me&nbsp;(M&amp;M) Soccer</li>
	<li>Speed, Agiligty and Conditioning (SAC)</li>
	<li>Competitive Soccer (TSC Hurricane)</li>
</ol>

<p>Additionally, MTsc has up to five tournaments per year (as part of it Challenger Series) including the largest 3v3 tournament in the nation (2 years running) and Oklahoma&#39;s largest college showcase.&nbsp; It also holds various Soccer Schools for free throughout the year.</p>

<p>To find out more about the Programs we offer, please choose the Program links both above and to your left.</p>

<p>If you have any questions feel free to email us at MTscParentsOnly@att.net (for parents) and all others at&nbsp;<a href="mailto:?metrotulsasoccer@att.net"><span style="color:rgb(0, 0, 205)">MetroTulsaSoccer@att.net</span></a>&nbsp;or call us at&nbsp;(918) 298-0190</p>
