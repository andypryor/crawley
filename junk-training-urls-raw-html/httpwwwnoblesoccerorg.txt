<h2>Upcoming events<br /></h2>
</div>
<div id="bk34" class="bk-widget widget-content widget-content-ASMV50">
<h3>Spring 2015 Registration Period is Open.</h3>
</div>
<div id="bk35" class="bk-widget widget-content widget-content-7FKM28">
<p><strong>Registration deadline is February 2, 2015.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Late Registration with late fee until February 9,
2015.&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><strong>Season starts March 7, 2015.</strong></p>
<p><strong><br /></strong></p>
<p><span style="color: #fd0c15; font-size: 14px;"><strong>In Person
Registrations:</strong></span></p>
<p><strong><span>Saturday January 31, 2015 at the Noble Public
Library. 10am-2pm</span></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="color: #00407f;"><strong><span style=
"font-size: 14px;">Online Registrations:</span></strong></span></p>
<p>We offer two soccer programs. &nbsp;The first is a
non-traveling, recreational program for younger players.
&nbsp;Practices are in Noble at the Hubbard Elementary School or
the Noble Soccer Complex (1/4 mile west of Hwy 77 &amp; Cemetery)
and all games are played in Norman at the Gillis-Rother Complex on
East Robinson. Teams are formed with same-aged youth from Noble and
the surrounding communities and typically coached by a parent of a
child on the team.&nbsp; Here is a direct link to the U-5 through
U-12 (non-traveling rec) program registration page.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style=
"font-size: 14px; font-family: georgia, palatino; color: rgb(0, 64, 127);">
<a href=
"https://www.gotsport.com/asp/application/easyreg/?ProgramID=34657"
target="_blank"><span style="color: rgb(0, 64, 127);">Click Here
for Online Rec Team Sign Ups</span></a></span></p>
<p>For older youth born between 8/1/95 and 7/31/02, we offer a
traveling rec soccer program. &nbsp;Traveling rec teams practice in
Noble (usually at Hubbard) and play home games at the Noble Soccer
Complex. &nbsp;Away games are played against opponents from all
over the OKC metro area and occasionally against teams from Lawton
or Stillwater. &nbsp;Teams are typically coached by more
experienced or licensed soccer coaches. &nbsp;Here is a direct link
to this program's registration page.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10px; color: rgb(0, 64, 127);"><a href=
"https://www.gotsport.com/asp/application/easyreg/?ProgramID=34657"
target="_blank"><span style="color: rgb(0, 64, 127);">Click Here
for Online Traveling Rec Team Sign Ups</span></a></span></p>

<h3>Volunteer Coaches<br /></h3>
</div>
<div id="bk43" class="bk-widget widget-content widget-content-929UZ6">
<p>The Club is always in need of willing, volunteer coaches for our
youth soccer program.&nbsp; To encourage increased participation,
the Coaching Discount will&nbsp;continue to be&nbsp;$35.&nbsp; All
new and returning coaches are provided training and coaching
materials as well as 100% reimbursement for completed coaching
courses administered by the Oklahoma Soccer Association.&nbsp; If
you are interested in becoming a coach or would like more
information about coaching, please contact our Coaching
Coordinator, Matt Maxwell, at
coachingcoordinator@noblesoccer.org.<br /></p>
