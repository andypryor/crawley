						<div class="advPubTkt-content"><p style="text-align: left;">
  The Idaho Olympic Development Program has selected four Sandpoint Strikers players to represent the state in January at the Regional Championships.
</p>
<p>
  <img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="http://www.sandpointsoccer.com/imagedata/IYSA.png" border="0" width="91" height="91" />
</p>
<p>
  Fourteen Strikers players were invited to the State Pool and from those fourteen nine attended the tryout for the final rosters. <strong>Curtis Hauck</strong> and <strong>Tyler Moore</strong>
  (Strikers BU14) were selected to represent the boys 2001 age group while <strong>Jezza Hutto</strong> and <strong>Riley Walkington</strong> (Strikers GU14) were selected for the girls 2001 age
  group.
</p>
<p>
  Idaho Youth Soccer Director of Coaching Skyler Bell was full of praise for Strikers players:
</p>
<p>
  <em>"The Strikers players who attended the tryout were all outstanding additions to Idaho ODP. The players invited to the ODP Championships are among the best players in Idaho and Eastern
  Washington, and a big kudos goes to the Strikers for being the key cog in their development." </em>
</p>
<p>
  The honor comes as a recognition for all the hard work the players, families, coaches, and volunteers put in for Sandpoint Strikers and they deserve a big THANK YOU for their many efforts.
</p>
<p>
  Congratulations, Strikers!
