<p><strong>Stillwater Soccer Club</strong></p>
<p>The Stillwater Soccer Club (SSC) is a non-profit, volunteer organization formed to promote the development of youth soccer in the Stillwater area. Our goal, each season, is to provide each player with an enjoyable soccer experience with participation, skill development and team camaraderie. Read more about our <a href="index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=80">Affiliations</a>, <a href="index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=80">Organization</a> and <a href="index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=80">Philosophy</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Babcock Park Soccer Complex</strong></p>
<p>Starting Fall 2014, all U-6 and U-8 Rec. games will no longer be played at Babcock Park Soccer Complex. They will now be played at our new facility at the Innovation Way.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.google.com/maps/dir//Babcock+Park,+Stillwater,+OK+74074/@36.104236,-97.090859,604m/data=!3m1!1e3!4m12!1m3!3m2!1s0x0:0x1e5fdc7d2c50569f!2sBabcock+Park!4m7!1m0!1m5!1m1!1s0x87b10a791d48b293:0x1e5fdc7d2c50569f!2m2!1d-97.090859!2d36.104236?hl=en-US" target="_blank">Get Directions to Babcock Park Soccer Complex.</a></p>
<p><img src="images/ssc/facilities/fields/babcock_park.png" border="1" alt="Babcock Park Fields" width="520" height="332" style="border: 1px solid black;" /></p>
<p><a href="index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=71">More information</a> about field assignments, field location and field number.</p>
<p><strong>Innovation Way Soccer Fields</strong></p>
