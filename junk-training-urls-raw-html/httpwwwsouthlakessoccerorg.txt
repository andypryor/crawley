            <div class="intro"><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 18.0pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: Ravie;">++NEW Program!++</span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 18.0pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: Ravie;">SLSC &ldquo;STREET SOCCER&rdquo;</span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;">What is it?</span></em></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">&ldquo;Street Soccer&rdquo; is backyard soccer where the players manage the game.&nbsp; There are no coaches or referees.&nbsp; Players decide on teams, formations, positions, etc.&nbsp; They must also call their own fouls and work together.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;">What is the Cost?</span></em></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">Players will pay $5 cash to participate at the field.&nbsp; This will allow players to attend without making a full commitment.&nbsp; Show up on the nights you can play and miss the others.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;">When? Where?</span></em></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">&ldquo;Street Soccer&rdquo; will be held on Friday nights from 730-830pm at the SLSC Game Complex.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">Players should bring all necessary equipment including a light/dark shirt.&nbsp; Shin-guards are required!</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;">Who?</span></em></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">All SLSC players are welcome to attend.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="font-size: 14.0pt; line-height: 115%;">Thoughts</span></em></strong><strong><em><span style="font-size: 18.0pt; line-height: 115%;">&hellip;</span></em></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">Too often soccer has way too much structure.&nbsp; Ask any good player at a high level what was vital in their development.&nbsp;&nbsp; Pick-up games of some kind in the backyard or park are always mentioned.&nbsp; We want the SLSC Game Complex to serve as the &ldquo;Backyard&rdquo; for our players.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><em><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; line-height: 115%;">Please come out and support the program and enjoy the game!</span></em></p>

        <div class="program_name">
            <a href='/Club'   >Club</a>        </div>
        <span class="date">

                <div class="copy">
            <div class="intro"><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 20.0pt; font-family: 'Bradley Hand ITC'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">***EXTRA TIME***</span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">The SLSC &ldquo;Extra Time&rdquo; Program is designed for all Competitive and Academy players.&nbsp; This Program is offered by the South Lakes Soccer Club to supplement the training received in the Competitive and Academy programs.&nbsp; Each program is tailored to the specific topic and the focus remains consistent throughout the program.&nbsp; Players will have 3 choices when it comes to training.&nbsp; These options are outlined below and players are welcome to participate in more than one session.&nbsp; Each program has its own unique benefits and will allow for maximum learning each session.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="font-size: 14.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">Speed &amp; Agility (530-630p-$50)</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">SLSC is dedicated to meeting all of its athlete&rsquo;s needs.&nbsp; The &ldquo;Speed &amp; Agility&rdquo; program is designed for all players interested in improving their overall athleticism.&nbsp; The sessions will focus on foot speed, (including transitions into cuts and changes of direction) foot coordination, acceleration, deceleration, core strength, and flexibility.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="font-size: 14.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">Ball Mastery (530-630p-$50)</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">In a team setting tactical and positional training must occur.&nbsp; Individual time spent with the ball tends to be less as players get older.&nbsp; Often players are looking for additional training or individual training to help in areas like speed, fast footwork, juggling, moves to beat opponents, and overall ball mastery.&nbsp; This program addresses the need for such technical training.&nbsp; While individual training will always be offered, the &ldquo;Ball Mastery&rdquo; program will offer a more affordable option.&nbsp; These hour long sessions will challenge players to take their technical skills to the next level.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="font-size: 14.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">Shooting &amp; Striking (630-730p-$50)</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">This very successful program will focus on proper technique in striking the soccer ball.&nbsp; Players often struggle with the ability to strike the ball correctly and with power.&nbsp; This program will address those needs.&nbsp; These sessions will be instructional and will allow players to grow in their individual confidence.&nbsp; There isn&rsquo;t anything better than striking a ball at goal!</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;There will be 8 sessions on Friday nights this season. &nbsp;Exact Dates will be coming soon!</span></p>

            <a href='/Recreational/news/show/spring_friday_night_head_start'   >Spring Friday Night Head Start</a>        </div>
        <div class="program_name">
            <a href='/Recreational'   >Recreational</a>        </div>
        <span class="date">
            2 weeks  ago
        </span>
                <div class="copy">
            <div class="intro"><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: center;" align="center"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: Algerian; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">SLSC &ldquo;Head Start&rdquo; Program</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;The SLSC Head Start Program is designed for all recreational players at any level.&nbsp; The Program is offered by the South Lakes Soccer Club to increase the technical skills of its participants while keeping things challenging and fun.&nbsp; These hour long sessions will challenge players to take their technical skills to the next level.&nbsp; Players will be sorted by age and ability.&nbsp; They will primarily be in these groups for the duration of the program.&nbsp; Specifically, they will work with the players on ball mastery, fast footwork, juggling, dribbling, dribbling to beat an opponent, dribbling for speed, passing, shooting, receiving, etc.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;This program will allow players to continue to play on their recreational team of choice while getting additional training that will help them take their game to the next level.&nbsp; This program will also offer coaches a great opportunity to have a professional staff work with their players and can be used as a practice for the entire team.&nbsp; Coaches are encouraged to attend, take notes, ask questions, and take ideas back to their teams on a regular basis.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman','serif'; mso-fareast-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;Fall Head Start Program- March 13th through May (8 sessions)</span></p>

            <a href='/Recreational/news/show/recreation_reschedule_policy'   >Recreation Reschedule Policy</a>        </div>
        <div class="program_name">
            <a href='/Recreational'   >Recreational</a>        </div>
        <span class="date">
            3 weeks  ago
        </span>
                <div class="copy">
            <div class="intro"><p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">SLSC Policy for Ages u5 &ndash; U12 In-House Recreational League</span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center"><span style="font-size: 14.0pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="text-decoration: underline;">This reschedule policy only applies to SLSC In-House recreational teams</span></em></strong>.&nbsp; All other leagues (Travel Recreation, Oklahoma Competitive League, and Oklahoma Premier League) must follow the OSA reschedule policy for their respective league.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">SLSC will attempt to accommodate all No Play and any special scheduling requests, but cannot guarantee every request will be accommodated due to possible scheduling constraints/challenges.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><em>Each coach must login to their GotSoccer coach account prior to the start of the season and update contact information to include a valid phone number and valid email address</em>.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Prior to season start and up to 48 hours after the pre-season coaches meeting, coaches can submit up to two (2) No Play dates (each request is one day) and any special scheduling requests to <a title="scheduler email" href="mailto:scheduler@southlakessoccer.org">scheduler@southlakessoccer.org</a> .&nbsp; The following information must be included with the request:</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Coach Name</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Team Name/Division</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Phone/email</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>IN-SEASON RESCHEDULE REQUESTS PROCEDURE</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong><em><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Coaches &ndash; check your schedule frequently, especially early in the season.</span></em></strong><em></em></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">All coach contact information will be located in the GotSoccer Schedule (Top right hand corner).</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Reschedule requests must be received by email at <a title="scheduler email" href="mailto:scheduler@southlakessoccer.org">scheduler@southlakessoccer.org</a>&nbsp; by Saturday, the week prior to your scheduled match to be considered for reschedule without additional fees applying.&nbsp; If you cancel/request a reschedule after this time or NO SHOW to a game, the team cancelling or no show will have to pay the referees in order to reschedule.&nbsp; The game will not be rescheduled until payment is received by SLSC.&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Fees range from $15 to $85 depending on division.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Game availability:</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Saturday - 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Sunday - 12:00 Noon to 5:00 PM</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Monday or Wednesday &ndash; 5:30 PM (available September, October, March, April, May)&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol;">&middot;<span style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 7pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]-->Contact the opposing coach to let them know you will need to reschedule your game and discuss potential reschedule dates.&nbsp; If you do not hear back from the other coach, please email <a title="scheduler email" href="mailto:scheduler@southlakessoccer.org">scheduler@southlakessoccer.org</a> for assistance.</p>
<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol;">&middot;<span style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 7pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]-->Email <a title="scheduler email" href="mailto:scheduler@southlakessoccer.org">scheduler@southlakessoccer.org</a> and (CC the opposing coach on all correspondence) to remove the game from the schedule and provide two new dates (preferred and secondary) and your preference for morning or afternoon. <strong>Both coaches must agree on the potential dates.</strong> &nbsp;<em>Please include the name of teams, game number, and original game date/time.</em></p>
<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol;">&middot;<span style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 7pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]-->Once a valid request is received SLSC will make every effort to accommodate the date/time range. Once a game has been rescheduled an email will be sent to both coaches notifying them of the change.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol;">&middot;<span style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 7pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><strong>Games left pending due to reschedule request but no follow through by coaches will be recorded as a 0-0 score.</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent: -.25in; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo1;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol;">&middot;<span style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 7pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span><!--[endif]--><strong>Games in which a team does not show and no attempt has been made to contact the opposing coach or an email has not been sent to </strong><a title="scheduler email" href="mailto:scheduler@southlakessoccer.org"><strong>scheduler@southlakessoccer.org</strong></a><strong> will be recorded as a forfeit 3-0.</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: .25in;">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;" align="center">Thank you,</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
