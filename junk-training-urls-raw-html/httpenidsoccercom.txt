			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://enidsoccer.com/2015/03/09/coaching-education-opportunities/" rel="bookmark">Coaching Education Opportunities</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://enidsoccer.com/2015/03/09/coaching-education-opportunities/" title="11:01 PM" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 9, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://enidsoccer.com/author/jeffp/" title="View all posts by Jeff Phillips">Jeff Phillips</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p>There are many education opportunities for coaches in Oklahoma.<br />
Enid Soccer Club will reimburse all fees after coaches have completed a course.</p>
<p><b>USSF National F License</b> [<a href="http://www.ussoccer.com/coaching-education/licenses/national-f" target="_blank">Details</a>]<br />
The &#8220;F&#8221; license is an online 2-hour course. It is intended for coaches of youth players.<br />
The focus of this course is to share best practices for coaching 5-8 year old players.<br />
The course is open to all applicants at least 16 years of age.</p>
<p><b>USSF National E License</b> [<a href="http://www.ussoccer.com/coaching-education/licenses/national-e" target="_blank">Details</a>]<br />
The &#8220;E&#8221; license courses are Friday 6pm-9pm and Sat/Sun 9am-6pm.<br />
&#8226;&nbsp; March 6-8 &#8212; USAO in Chickasha<br />
&#8226;&nbsp; March 27-29 &#8212; Bethany at SCU<br />
&#8226;&nbsp; April 10-12 &#8212; Enid, OK</p>
<p><b>USSF National C License</b> [<a href="http://www.oksoccer.com/coaching/youthlicense.aspx" target="_blank">Details</a>]<br />
&#8226;&nbsp; July 30-August 2 &#8212; OKC<br />
&#8226;&nbsp; August 6-9 &#8212; OKC</p>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://enidsoccer.com/2015/03/03/enid-high-soccer/" title="6:32 PM" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 3, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://enidsoccer.com/author/jeffp/" title="View all posts by Jeff Phillips">Jeff Phillips</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Friday, April 10th, is Enid High&#8217;s ESC coach appreciation night. All ESC coaches, on the guest list, get free admission. The coaches will be recognized at half time of the varsity games for their efforts in growing the game and developing young players. To be added to the coach guest list, please email <a href="mailto:ctliddell@enidk12.org">Craig Liddell</a> by April 8th.</p>
<p>The full <a target="_blank" href="http://www.enidpublicschools.org/education/components/calendar/calendar.php?sectiondetailid=27559">2015 EHS soccer schedule</a> is available online.</p>
<p>Enid Soccer Club players get <b>free admission</b> to home games.<br />
To qualify, players must be in uniform and accompanied by a paying adult.</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://enidsoccer.com/2015/02/09/soccer-clinic-with-a-former-pro-soccer-player/" rel="bookmark">Soccer Clinic with a Former Pro Soccer Player</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://enidsoccer.com/2015/02/09/soccer-clinic-with-a-former-pro-soccer-player/" title="8:55 PM" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">February 9, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://enidsoccer.com/author/jeffp/" title="View all posts by Jeff Phillips">Jeff Phillips</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p>We are very excited to announce that we are hosting an OSA Soccer Clinic with former pro soccer player <b>Jared Montz</b>. This OSA Soccer Clinic is <b>March 17-19</b> for players ages 6-12. Sessions will be in the afternoon at the soccer complex. </p>
<p>Jared Montz is a former MLS player and founder of <a href="http://www.OnlineSoccerAcademy.com" target="_blank">OnlineSoccerAcademy.com</a> (OSA).  At the Online Soccer Academy they make better players through free online training videos.  Players all over the world watch OSA videos to improve their game.  OSA videos have been viewed over 16 million times!  Jared and his team&#8217;s mission is to teach players that if you &#8220;Believe in it&#8221; and back that up with hard work, anything in life is possible.  </p>
<p>Besides coaching players through video, Jared and his team do OSA soccer camps and clinics all around the country.  We are excited to host them in Enid!</p>
<p><b>Register and pay online</b>:<br />
<a target="_blank" href="http://www.OnlineSoccerAcademy.com/soccer-camps/osa-soccer-clinic-enid-oklahoma">OnlineSoccerAcademy.com/soccer-camps/osa-soccer-clinic-enid-oklahoma</a><br />
Limited spots available, sign up quick to reserve yours!</p>
<p>Jared Montz<br />
(985) 237-0763<br />
<a href="mailto:jared@onlinesocceracademy.com">jared@onlinesocceracademy.com</a></p>
<p>We look forward to seeing you at the OSA Soccer Clinic!</p>
