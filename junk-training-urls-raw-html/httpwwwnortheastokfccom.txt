<p><span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;"><strong>COLORADO</strong> --&nbsp; Fresh from a State Cup semi-final appearance to complete the 2013-2014 competitive season, the NEOFC 02 Boys Blue embarked on a mission to combine on-pitch achievement with a week of family fun.&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;">By the end of their trip the 02 Blues would leave their mark on &ldquo;Colorful Colorado,&rdquo; bringing back tournament title hardware, along with treasured soccer family memories.</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.6em;">After making a way through the Great Plains, the acclimation process for the &rsquo;02 Boys&rsquo; extended stay in The Centennial State would begin by taking part in the Rush Open at Dick&rsquo;s Sporting Goods Park in Commerce City.&nbsp;&nbsp; There, at the home of the MLS&rsquo; Colorado Rapids, Coach Brunner&rsquo;s boys would recover from an early setback against the host Colorado Rush by defeating the CASA Thunders 2-0 in a consolation game on June 8</span><sup style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; line-height: 1.6em;">th</sup><span style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.6em;">.&nbsp; The boys were able to get out in front before an electrical storm would bring an early end to the game and tournament.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:14px;"><span style="font-family:verdana,geneva,sans-serif;"><img alt="" src="http://www.northeastokfc.com/imgs/centerarticles/redrocks.jpg" style="width: 225px; height: 127px; float: right; margin: 7px;" /></span></span></p>

<div><span style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.6em;">The team gathered to train at the home of the Colorado Ice youth soccer club on Thursday June 12</span><sup style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; line-height: 1.6em;">th</sup><span style="font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.6em;">.&nbsp; The 02 Blues enjoyed exercises on the artificial turf and scenic grounds in preparation for the Rocky Mountain Cup in Golden, Colorado that weekend.</span></div>
