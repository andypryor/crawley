						<p>Middleton soccer club is a 501c3 non-profit organization dedicated to providing the children of Middleton with the opportunity to participate in the sport of soccer.</p>
<p><strong>Short Description:</strong><br />
The Middleton Soccer club is located in Middleton Idaho. We run leagues in the Spring and Fall Seasons.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>GOALS OF THE PROGRAM</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>Increase children&#8217;s self-esteem through participation in an organized and supportive program of team ac<span class="text_exposed_show">tivities<br />
Build positive social and life skills<br />
Provide important exercise and increase awareness of one&#8217;s own health through sports<br />
Provide programs that are not available in the schools<br />
Provide programs to encourage children that sports are an alternative to crime and can keep them off the streets</span></p>
