<p><span style="font-size: small;">Idaho Thunder FC is a non-profit organization run by volunteers to offer an ethical, positive, and competitive soccer program for the youth of North Idaho and Eastern Washington.&nbsp; Supporting training and enrichment in technical, tactical, physical, and mental aspects of soccer.&nbsp; We also support the teaching of the importance of integrity, respect, commitment, teamwork, sportsmanship, and community involvement.</span></p>

<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">Keep players safe and healthy.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">Continued support to develop players' skills and knowledge of the game of soccer.</span></li>

<li><span style="font-size: small;">Provide quality coaching to nurture and develop players through technical and tactical practices along with the highest level of game competition.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">To promote high standards of sportsmanship and fair play.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Our Values:</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">EVERY PLAYER MATTERS!</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">We promote respect of teammates, coaches, referees, and competitors.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">To gain support and participation of our parents, alumni, and community.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small;">All decisions will be made with integrity and honesty.</span></li>
